 EXPLANATION
 -----------
 THE AMADEUS INFORMATIVE PRICING TRANSACTION ALLOWS YOU TO PRICE
 AN ITINERARY, WITHOUT FIRST CREATING A PNR. THIS IS ESPECIALLY
 USEFUL AT TIMES WHEN YOU NEED TO FIND THE PRICE FOR AN
 ITINERARY, BUT YOU ARE NOT YET READY TO MAKE A RESERVATION.
 
 AS NO PNR IS CREATED, PROCESSING IS LIMITED TO THE
 CONSIDERATION OF DATA ENTERED BY THE USER. DATA SPECIFIED IN
 THE INPUT AND ALSO STORED ELSEWHERE IN THE SYSTEM WILL NOT BE
 CHECKED WHEN PROCESSING THE REQUEST, FOR EXAMPLE, THERE IS NO
>
m
                        INFORMATIVE PRICING   EN  28FEB25 2237Z
 INPUT OPTION TO IDENTIFY UNTICKETED INTERMEDIATE POINTS OF A
 FLIGHT, EVEN IF FLIGHT NUMBERS ARE INCLUDED, THE FARE SERVER
 WILL NOT CHECK FLIFO.
 
 THE ACCURACY OF THE RESPONSE IS DEPENDENT ON THE ACCURACY OF
 THE DATA PROVIDED IN THE INPUT.
 
 
                                                             >MD
 
 IN THE EVENT THAT DATA ELEMENTS NORMALLY CONTAINED IN A PNR
 PRICING REQUEST ARE MISSING, THE PROCESSING APPLIES ASSUMPTIONS
 FOR EXAMPLE, IF THE DATE IS SPECIFIED BUT FLIGHT TIMES ARE NOT,
 THE SYSTEM WILL ASSUME THE ARRIVAL IS ON THE SAME DAY.
 
 AS THE DISTRIBUTION SYSTEM DOES NOT STORE ALL FARE-RELATED
 INFORMATION INCLUDING ANY DATA IN HISTORICAL FILES, ALL
 REQUESTS FOR PRESENT OR PAST-DATE TAX DATA MAY RESULT IN
 INCORRECT CALCULATION OF TAXES.
 
 SIMILARLY, ANY PAST-DATE PRICING MAY ALSO BE SUBJECT TO
 INCORRECT CALCULATION OF MILEAGE AND HIP CHECKS. THE RESULT MAY
 ALSO BE AFFECTED BY CHANGES IN THE FILING OF THE FARE OR RULE
>
m
                        INFORMATIVE PRICING   EN  28FEB25 2237Z
 SINCE THE TICKETING DATE.
 
 YOU CAN ADD OPTIONS TO YOUR ENTRY, OR YOU CAN CREATE A MASK
 THAT CAN GUIDE YOU TO ADD OPTIONS. YOU CAN ALSO CALCULATE
 DISCOUNT FARES, INCLUDING CUMULATIVE DISCOUNTS.
 
                                                             >MD
 
 THE FOLLOWING CONDITIONS APPLY:
 
 - YOU CAN USE FQP FOR ALL FARES EXCEPT GROUP FARES AND AIR
   PASSES.
 
 - FQP IS APPLICABLE FOR PAST DATE FARES. SEE PAST DATE PRICING
   LATER IN THIS SECTION FOR DETAILED INFORMATION.
 
 A ROUND OR CIRCLE TRIP CAN CONTAIN A MAXIMUM OF:
  -  SIXTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS, OR
  -  FOURTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS AND TWO SURFACE SEGMENTS OR,
  -  FIFTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS AND ONE SURFACE ELEMENT OR,
  -  FIFTEEN FARE BREAK POINTS
 
 A ONE WAY TRIP CAN CONTAIN A MAXIMUM OF:
>
m
                        INFORMATIVE PRICING   EN  28FEB25 2237Z
  -  FIFTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS, OR
  -  THIRTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS AND TWO SURFACE SEGMENTS OR,
  -  FOURTEEN FLIGHT SEGMENTS AND ONE SURFACE ELEMENT OR,
  -  FOURTEEN FARE BREAK POINTS
                                                             >MD
 
 IF YOU DO NOT INCLUDE ANY SPECIFIC OPTIONS IN YOUR ENTRY, THE
 SYSTEM MAKES THE FOLLOWING ASSUMPTIONS:
 
  - AN ADULT PASSENGER TYPE
  - THE CURRENT DATE FOR THE FIRST SEGMENT, AND AN OPEN DATE FOR
    ALL OTHER SEGMENTS.
  - AN OPEN BOOKING CLASS. THE SYSTEM DISPLAYS THE LOWEST FARE
    FOR EACH FARE TYPE.
  - YY FARES FOR TRAVEL AGENCIES, AND CARRIER SPECIFIC FARES FOR
    AIRLINES.
  - STOPOVERS AT ALL CITIES.

 A LIST OF FARES (/P). FOR EXAMPLE, TO REQUEST A MASK FOR THE

 CURRENCY OF FARE SELECTION OVERRIDE
 -----------------------------------
 WHEN A FARE IS FILED IN MORE THAN ONE CURRENCY, IT IS RETURNED
 IN THE DEFAULT FILING CURRENCY FOR THE MARKET. FOR EXAMPLE, FOR
 SOME AIRLINES, ONLY INR FARES ARE SELECTED FOR INDIAN DOMESTIC
 JOURNEYS SOLD IN INDIA, WHILE ONLY USD FARES ARE SELECTED IF
 SOLD OUTSIDE INDIA.
 
 FOR PUBLIC DOMESTIC FARES FILED IN MORE THAN ONE CURRENCY, YOU
 CAN OVERRIDE THE CURRENCY OF FARE SELECTION TO USD, EUROS OR
 THE LOCAL CURRENCY, PROVIDED THAT THE FARE HAS BEEN FILED IN
 THAT CURRENCY.
 
 CONTINUING THE EXAMPLE ABOVE, THIS MEANS THAT YOU CAN:
 - SELL USD FARES IN INDIA TO NON-INDIAN RESIDENTS NOT ELIGIBLE
   FOR THE INR FARES
 - SELL INR FARES IN NEPAL/BHUTAN TO NEPALESE/BHUTANESE
   RESIDENTS ELIGIBLE FOR THESE FARES.

 STOPOVERS AND TRANSFERS
 -----------------------
 THE TERM TRANSFER REFERS TO A CONNECTION. IF A ONE-WAY
 ITINERARY CONTAINS MORE THAN ONE SEGMENT, OR IF A ROUND-TRIP
 ITINERARY CONTAINS MORE THAN TWO SEGMENTS, AMADEUS ASSUMES A
 TRANSFER IF THE DURATION AT A CITY IS WITHIN THE FOLLOWING:
 
 - UP TO 4 HOURS IN THE US, CANADA, PUERTO RICO AND VIRGIN
   ISLANDS, FOR WHOLLY DOMESTIC ITINERARIES.
 - UP TO 12 HOURS IN THE US, CANADA, PUERTO RICO AND
   VIRGIN ISLANDS WHEN PARTS OF AN ITINERARY IS INTERNATIONAL.
   FOR EXAMPLE: INT-INT/INT-DOM V.V
 - THE SAME DAY IN AUSTRALIA AND NEW ZEALAND FOR WHOLLY
   DOMESTIC ITINERARIES.
 - UP TO 24 HOURS EVERYWHERE ELSE.
 
 THE SYSTEM PRICES TO ALLOW STOPOVERS AT ALL LOCATIONS, IF
 NOTHING IS SPECIFIED. HOWEVER, IN SOME CASES YOU MAY NEED TO
 OVERRIDE THE STOPOVER ASSUMPTION.

 STOPOVER AND TRANSFER INDICATOR (-)
 -----------------------------------
 IF A STOPOVER INDICATOR IS NOT USED IN THE INPUT, THEN ALL
 POINTS ARE CONSIDERED TO BE POTENTIAL STOPOVERS. IF DATES OR
 TIMES ARE INCLUDED IN THE INPUT, THE SYSTEM WILL AUTOMATICALLY
 DETERMINE IF POINTS ARE STOPOVERS OR TRANSFERS.
 
 TO OVERRIDE SYSTEM DETERMINATION OF A STOPOVER OR TRANSFER,
 ENTER A HYPHEN (-) AFTER EACH CITY YOU WOULD LIKE TO INDICATE
 AS A STOPOVER. FOR EXAMPLE, IF YOU ENTER:
 
                    FQP MADGVACPH-FRAZRHBUD
 
 CPH IS CONSIDERED A STOPOVER, WHILE THE OTHER CITIES IN THE
 ITINERARY ARE TRANSFERS, (EXCEPT THE TERMINAL POINT).
